Welcome to Chuki's Grill Hawaiian BBQ

Hawaii is a melting pot of ethnic cultures and nowhere is this influence more profound than in its cusine. At Chuki's Grill Hawaiian BBQ, we specialize in a delicious selection of “Plate Lunch” and other Hawaiian inspired foods. We are passionate about combining the culture spirit of Hawaii into our restaurants to bring you a taste of the islands with exceptional service in an inviting atmosphere.The key to being Chuki's Grill is using fresh ingredients prepared daily and making our dishes in house with our delicious recipes. We make our marinades from scratch and marinate our meats everyday for that perfect flavor. We hand roll our Katsu in panko bread crumbs and make our soups fresh daily.
